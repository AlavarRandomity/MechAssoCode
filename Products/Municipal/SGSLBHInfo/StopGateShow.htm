Stop Gates

Mechanical Associates fabricated Stop Gates are built to engineered specifications per project. Stop Gates are fabricated from stainless steel or aluminum. Stop Gates are manually lifted gates that are installed either in open channels or the end of a channel/pipe. Stop gates are used for blocking or diverting flow through channels or the end of a pipe run. Smaller gates can be referred to as hand pull stop gates. Larger gates often require a mechanical lifting device such as a hoist or crane. The term “Stop gates”, “Stop Plates”, “Hand Pull Stop Gates” can be used interchangeably.
