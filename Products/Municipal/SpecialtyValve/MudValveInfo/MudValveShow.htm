Mud Valves

Mechanical Associates fabricated Mud Valves are built to engineered specifications per project. Mud valves are fabricated from Stainless Steel and with a 150# flanged end connections in sizes 3" - 48". Mud valves have the option of either a rising stem or a non-rising stem and can be operated using any valve control method (actuator, hand wheel, T-handle, etc.). Mud valves are designed for settling basin drain lines, sump blow-offs, swimming pool drains, waterworks, sewage and filtration plants, irrigation systems, and industrial installations.
