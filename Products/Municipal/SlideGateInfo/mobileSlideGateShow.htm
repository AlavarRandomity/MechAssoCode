AWWA Fabricated Slide Gates

Light - Medium - Heavy Duty

Mechanical Associates' fabricated slide gates are built to engineered specifications per project. Sizes range from 3”x3” up to 240”x240”. We also offer our standard gate sizes that are designed for 10 feet of on and off-seating head pressures. Both the FC-SS and the UHO-10-AL series can be designed for any installation method and seal configuration in projects ranging from municipal to irrigation. For a complete listing of applications and configurations download the product brochure. Mechanical associates can also custom engineer any gate to meet our customer's needs.
