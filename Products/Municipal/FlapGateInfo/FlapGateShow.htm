Flap Gates

Standard Duty - Heavy Duty

Mechanical Associates' Flap Gates are made of all Stainless Steel or Aluminum construction for sustainability and longevity and utilizes a replaceable, adjustable, neoprene seal that provides zero leakage as well as a cushion for the flap to seat on for quiet operation.

Flap Gates are used for a variety of applications from Flood Control to Irrigation and are available in sizes up to 120” or custom larger gates. Mechanical Associates' Flap Gates are designed to open and close with very minimal differential pressure from either side of the gate. Our flap gates can also be designed for a variety of mounting configurations including flat back (wall or thimble mounted), flange back (flange to flange), and spigot back (pipe mounting). We can also design for special mounting configurations such as curved surfaces.
